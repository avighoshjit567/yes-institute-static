Diploma in Hotel Management

Complete hospitality training for students who want careers in hotel operations, guest service, food service, housekeeping, and hospitality management.

Apply for this course

What you will learn

This diploma gives students a broad foundation in hotel departments and day-to-day hospitality operations. It is designed for learners who want practical career preparation with professional behavior, service standards, and management basics.

  • Front office operations, guest handling, reservations, and communication
  • Food and beverage service techniques for restaurant and banquet settings
  • Housekeeping operations, room standards, hygiene, and safety
  • Hospitality supervision, teamwork, grooming, and workplace discipline

Course summary

Best for: Hotel and hospitality career seekers

Mode: Practical and classroom training

Career paths: Front office, F & B service, housekeeping, hotel operations
