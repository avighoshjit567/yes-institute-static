Certificate Program in F & B Production 1 Year

A professional chef training program with culinary fundamentals, production skills, menu preparation, kitchen safety, and extended practical work.

What you will learn

This one-year certificate is built for students who want a deeper preparation for professional kitchen work. The course focuses on repeated practice, kitchen confidence, and job-ready food production habits.

  • Basic to advanced cooking techniques for commercial kitchens
  • Food preparation, mise en place, plating, and presentation
  • Kitchen hygiene, safety, equipment handling, and storage standards
  • Bangladeshi, continental, and practical menu production training
